Our Story

FurniFind was established in 2018 with a clear vision: to offer modern, thoughtfully designed furniture that enhances living spaces. What began as a boutique showroom has evolved into a destination for interior enthusiasts and homeowners seeking quality craftsmanship and timeless design.

Our story started when our founder, Alex Morgan, spent years studying furniture design across Scandinavia and Japan. Inspired by the minimalist aesthetics and functional beauty encountered, Alex created FurniFind as a space where people could experience furniture that perfectly balances form and function.

Today, FurniFind represents our dedication to quality materials, sustainable practices, and creating furniture that transforms houses into homes.

Our Values

Sustainability

We source our furniture through direct relationships with manufacturers who practice sustainable production and use responsibly sourced wood and materials.

Quality

We meticulously select each piece in our collection to ensure that every furniture item meets our exacting standards of craftsmanship excellence.

Community

We believe beautiful spaces bring people together. Our showrooms are designed to foster connection, conversation, and the shared joy of home decoration and design discovery.

Innovation

We're committed to promoting innovative design and functionality through our design workshops, furniture showcases, and educational programs for interior enthusiasts of all ages.
